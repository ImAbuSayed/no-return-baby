[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook 1]
Akhiyan de kaale jaadu mind blown kudiye,
Saari raat takda haan face tera heeriye,
Naina di khumari aisi wild soul jaani,
Dil mera luteya ae rule breaker raani.

[Hook 2]
Hass ke tu jaan kadd lai slow mo chaal,
Seene vich agg laave burnin' nakhra kamaal,
Bulla'n chon nasha dullda heavy dose pyaar,
Hosh main gawa betha losing mind yaar.

[Verse 1]
Kaali kaali raat aayi kaisa moonlit shadow,
Sheesheyan 'ch disda ae tera neon glow,
Teri ik takni 'ch froze time mera,
Duniya nu bhul gya haan toxic crime tera.
Chandi de libaas vich kaisa silver flash,
Hawa vich udd'di ae banke fire ash,
Mere val aave tu tor moving slow,
Tenu main takda haan deep underflow.

[Pre-Chorus]
Saahan di garmi de vich melt down hove,
Doori hun thodi vi na heartbeat rove,
Khwaaban vich aave tu banke secret escape,
Ishq da nasha chade varga pure landscape.

[Chorus]
Tera no return baby mera rukda ni saah,
Ankhan vich akhan paake heart attack raah,
Tera no return baby tenu takeya main jadon,
Sari raat nachna ae run the town kado,
Jad vi tu aundi ae stop sign kudiye,
Hosh main gawa dinda borderline kudiye,
Ishq 'ch no return baby endless fall,
Tere piche pagal haan main breakin' the wall.

[Verse 2]
Gaddiyan di cheekh sun kaisa heavy metal roar,
Tere piche ghumda haan band locked out door,
Kacheyan de wangu na main koi weak link haan,
Pakka mera pyar sohniye main iron grip haan.
Agg wangu balda haan main burnin' flame,
Dil naal khed da ni koi silly game,
Vairiyan nu karda haan pal vich silent mute,
Tere layi dhadakda haan banke loyal absolute.

Ajj badleya mausam vi banke toxic storm,
Tere mere ishq da aivein rebel new form,
Yaadan vich jalda haan balda frozen fire,
Jadon di tu takkeya ae jaagi midnight desire.
Zehar wangu vajda ae banke deadly snake,
Dil te nishane vajde every single ache,
Tera mera rishta ae kaisa blindfold chase,
Zindagi lutaunda haan main teri dark maze.

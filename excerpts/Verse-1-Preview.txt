Kaali kaali raat aayi kaisa moonlit shadow,
Sheesheyan 'ch disda ae tera neon glow,
Teri ik takni 'ch froze time mera,
Duniya nu bhul gya haan toxic crime tera.
Chandi de libaas vich kaisa silver flash,
Hawa vich udd'di ae banke fire ash,
Mere val aave tu tor moving slow,
Tenu main takda haan deep underflow.

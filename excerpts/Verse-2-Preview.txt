Gaddiyan di cheekh sun kaisa heavy metal roar,
Tere piche ghumda haan band locked out door,
Kacheyan de wangu na main koi weak link haan,
Pakka mera pyar sohniye main iron grip haan.
Agg wangu balda haan main burnin' flame,
Dil naal khed da ni koi silly game,
Vairiyan nu karda haan pal vich silent mute,
Tere layi dhadakda haan banke loyal absolute.
